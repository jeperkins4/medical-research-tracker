## 3) Who this is for
- Bladder cancer patients and families managing complex care
- Caregivers coordinating labs, meds, and appointments
- Patients using genomics-informed treatment discussions

---

## 4) Core value proposition
Most people juggle portals, PDFs, notes apps, and memory.
MRT brings it together in one local-first app:
- Track labs and trends
- Organize medications/supplements
- Keep genomic findings connected to treatment context
- Run research scanning workflows and preserve findings
- Generate strategy summaries for clinical conversations

---

## 5) What’s new in v0.1.68
- Replaced blocking alert popups with in-app status messaging in key flows
- Improved desktop-mode clarity where API bridges are still in progress
- Readability improvements for small text
- Better navigation behavior at tighter window widths
- Keyboard focus-visible improvements for accessibility
- Header compaction for more usable screen space

---

## 6) 90-second demo script
1. Open MRT and show Overview/Treatment tabs
2. Show medication + research context in one place
3. Show genomics section and why it matters for discussions with oncologists
4. Show research workflow and saved papers
