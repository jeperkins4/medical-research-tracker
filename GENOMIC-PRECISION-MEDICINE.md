### 3Ô∏è‚É£ **Immune Escape/PD-L1** (Immunotherapy Resistance)

**Mutation involved**: ARID1A loss ‚Üí PD-L1 upregulation

**The problem**:
- PD-L1 on cancer cells binds PD-1 on T-cells

**Why this matters**:
ARID1A loss makes your tumor more immunogenic (attracts immune cells) BUT also upregulates PD-L1. This is **why Keytruda works for you** - it blocks the PD-L1 brake.

**Strategy**: 
Enhance Keytruda efficacy by:
1. Boosting T-cell function (immune support)
2. Increasing tumor antigen presentation
3. Reducing immunosuppressive cells (MDSCs, Tregs)

**Genomic-targeted interventions**:
