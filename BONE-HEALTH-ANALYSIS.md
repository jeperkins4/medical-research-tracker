## Critical Gaps - What's Missing ‚ö†Ô∏è

### 1. **Vitamin D3 - INSUFFICIENT DOSE**
**Current:** 1000 IU daily
**Needed for bone mets prevention:** 4,000-10,000 IU daily
**Why:** 
- Vitamin D is critical for bone health
- Anti-cancer effects (inhibits proliferation, induces apoptosis)
- Modulates immune system
- Studies show higher doses needed for cancer patients

**Recommendation:** Increase to 5,000 IU daily minimum (test serum 25-OH Vitamin D)

### 2. **Vitamin K2 (MK-7) - MISSING**
**Needed:** 100-200 mcg daily
**Why:**
- Activates osteocalcin (directs calcium to bones, not arteries)
- Works synergistically with Vitamin D3
- May inhibit cancer cell growth
- Critical for preventing arterial calcification (important with Eliquis)

**Recommendation:** Add Vitamin K2-MK7 100-200mcg daily

### 3. **Calcium - MISSING**
**Needed:** 1000-1200 mg daily (with K2 to prevent arterial calcification)
**Why:**
- Essential for bone density
- May reduce bone resorption in metastatic disease
- Must be paired with K2 and D3 for proper utilization

**Recommendation:** Add calcium citrate 1200mg daily (split doses)

### 4. **Bisphosphonates or Denosumab - NOT PRESCRIBED**
**Evidence-based for bladder cancer bone mets:**
- **Zoledronic acid** (Zometa) - IV, monthly
- **Denosumab** (Xgeva) - subcutaneous injection, monthly
**Why:**
- Inhibit osteoclast activity (reduce bone breakdown)
- Reduce skeletal-related events (SRE)
- Lower ALP levels when successful
- TUGAMO study showed efficacy in bladder cancer

**Recommendation:** **URGENT - Discuss with Dr. Do at next appointment**

### 5. **Boron - MISSING**
**Needed:** 3-6 mg daily
**Why:**
- Improves calcium/magnesium metabolism
- Increases Vitamin D activation
- Anti-inflammatory
- May inhibit prostate cancer (limited bladder cancer data)

**Recommendation:** Add boron 3mg daily

### 6. **Magnesium - STATUS UNKNOWN**
**Needed:** 400-500 mg daily
**Why:**
- Required for Vitamin D activation
- Critical for bone formation
- Works with calcium (1:2 ratio with calcium)

**Recommendation:** Check if supplementing, add 400mg magnesium glycinate if not

### 7. **Strontium - POTENTIAL**
**Consideration:** Strontium citrate 680mg daily
**Why:**
- Increases bone formation
- Decreases bone resorption
- Used in osteoporosis
- Limited cancer data but promising mechanism

**Recommendation:** Research further, discuss with oncology team
