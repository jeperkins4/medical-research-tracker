# Trusted Supplement Sources

## Quality Standards

For cancer treatment protocols, supplement quality is **critical**. Look for:

✅ **Third-party tested** (USP, NSF, ConsumerLab)  
✅ **cGMP certified** (FDA current Good Manufacturing Practice)  
✅ **Certificate of Analysis (COA)** available  
✅ **Heavy metal testing** (especially important for cancer patients)  
✅ **No fillers/binders** that could interfere with treatment

## Red Flags (Avoid)

❌ **No COA available** (can't verify purity)  
❌ **Proprietary blends** without ingredient amounts  
❌ **Made in China** (heavy metal contamination risk)  
❌ **Amazon brands with no third-party testing** (counterfeits common)  
❌ **MLM brands** (overpriced, questionable quality)  
❌ **"Cure cancer" claims** (FDA violation, not trustworthy)
